Frequently Asked Questions
Who can join Developer Student Community?
We are an open community of developers, anybody with a similar domain and interest can join us.
Is it free of cost?
Joining us is totally free of cost !
Where can I connect with Developer Student Community?
We can be reached out on several social media platforms including LinkedIn, Discord, Twitter, YouTube, Instagram etc.
What kind of workshops does Developer Student Community organize?
We have previously organized workshops on machine learning, career opportunities, Python, development etc.
How can I contribute?
Join us on any of the platforms and get in touch with us according to requirements. We’re open to learning!
What are the other perks that I get?
•Active organisers of Webinar’s which you can attend from any corner of the world.
•Adequate updates and information on several competitions and hackathons from different parts of the world for our members.
•Improve your networking by interactive sessions with experienced people from the field.
I am a beginner who has no knowledge about development. Can I still join?
Our community is growing with a rapid speed because we welcome newcomers with great enthusiasm. Learn and grow with us. It’s easier to write on a blank paper after all.
